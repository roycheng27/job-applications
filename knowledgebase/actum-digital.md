## Experience Scope
Contributed to AI-driven knowledge management tools and internal productivity systems. Worked with data scientists, consultants, engineers, and PMs to develop an internal AI assistant platform that ingests internal documents, creates structured metadata, embeds content into a vector database, and enables AI-assisted retrieval and workflow support. ACTUM's internal knowledge and project information was scattered across documents, files, workflows, and team systems, creating friction for employees finding info, generating artifacts, creating user stories, or reusing prior knowledge. The need: AI-enabled internal tools to centralize organizational knowledge, improve retrieval, reduce manual document processing, support workflow automation, help generate structured outputs, improve operational scalability, and lay a foundation for future AI agent ecosystems.
