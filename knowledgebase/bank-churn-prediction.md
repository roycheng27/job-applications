## Business Problem
Banks lose revenue when customers churn. Predicting churn allows identification of high-risk customers, understanding of drivers, and design of targeted retention strategies before customers leave. Objectives: identify key churn factors; build predictive models; improve recall for high-risk customers; translate findings into actionable retention strategies.
