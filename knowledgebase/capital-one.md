## Business / Organizational Context
Capital One operates large, complex internal organizations that undergo structural changes, team transitions, talent reallocation, and strategic workforce planning. Leaders need reliable ways to evaluate whether organizational changes are working. The analytical challenge is not just reporting descriptive workforce metrics, but defining what successful transitions look like, identifying measurable success indicators, and creating insights that help internal decision-makers assess retention, mobility, talent development, team stability, productivity, engagement, and operational effectiveness.
