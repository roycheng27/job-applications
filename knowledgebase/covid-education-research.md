## Experience Scope
Data analysis and visualization project studying how the COVID pandemic affected secondary education outcomes. Analyzed student academic performance across 2019–2023, examining test-score trends across demographics, geographies, and education policy contexts. Roy contributed to data collection, cleaning, merging, visualization, time-series analysis, and Tableau map development for a research website.

## Research / Business Problem
The pandemic disrupted student learning across the U.S. The project aimed to understand how secondary education outcomes changed during and after the pandemic. Key questions: How did performance change across years? Which geographies experienced larger declines? How did test scores vary across demographics/policy contexts? How could results be communicated through interactive visuals?
