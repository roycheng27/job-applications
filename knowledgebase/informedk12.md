## Experience Scope
Led a team of 6 consultants on an expansion strategy project for InformedK12, researching potential market entry opportunities across private higher education, public higher education, and city governments. The team conducted literature reviews, data analysis, stakeholder outreach, expert interviews, competitor benchmarking, and final recommendation development. Roy managed workstreams, guided research, and delivered client-facing materials.

## Business Problem
InformedK12 needed to evaluate potential expansion markets and determine which verticals offered viable opportunities. The client needed structured research and strategic guidance for market entry decision-making — where to expand beyond core markets and how to position against competitors.
